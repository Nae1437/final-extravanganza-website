Latest Releases

Most recent albums by Hannah Bahng include The Misunderstood EP and The Abysmal EP. Both albums come after the release of Perfect Blues, the first album she ever released.

Tour Information

Hannah as a more recent artist has gone on tour throughout North America two times. Her first major tour was titled The Abysmal Tour in late 2024. Similarly her second major tour was in late 2025 titled The Misunderstood Tour.
