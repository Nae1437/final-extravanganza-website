Fun Facts

While Hannah Bahng is becoming increasingly popular, there are still many things about her that people may not know- Hannah Bahng has an older brother named Bang Chan (Christopher Bahng) who is the leader of K-Pop Group Stray Kids, Hannah was sought out by Hybe Labels to be part of the Global Girl Group Katseye, Hannah also has an obsession with Pokemon and Adventure Time thus eludes to them in music videos and songs.

If Not This, Then What?

If Hannah Bahng did not pursue a singing career as a soloist with her own company, she has discussed that when she was younger she saw herself becoming a K-Pop Idol, similarly to how her older brother has as a member of Stray Kids.

Family Dynamic

Hannah Bahng has a family of five with two siblings. She is considered the middle child as she has both an older and a younger brother.
